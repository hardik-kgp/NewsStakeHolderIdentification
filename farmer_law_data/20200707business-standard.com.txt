Topics farmers ' protest | Supreme Court Press Trust of India | New Delhi Last Updated at January 17 , 2021 23:19 IST https : //mybs.in/2ZbJLKS Agitating farmer unions on Sunday remained firm on holding a tractor rally on the Republic Day and vowed to continue their stir till the agri laws are repealed , even as Agriculture Minister Narendra Singh Tomar urged them to discuss alternatives to scrapping the legislation at the next meeting scheduled for January 19 .
`` We are prepared to sit in protest till May 2024 ... Our demand is that the three laws be taken back and the government provide a legal guarantee on the MSP , '' Bharatiya Kisan Union ( BKU ) leader Rakesh Tikait told reporters in Nagpur .
